Our Vision

We started Ushur with a vision to simplify the way businesses engage their customers. Enterprises are moving service management to the Cloud; but the new solutions are missing real-time mobile engagement through self-service workflows. We developed the Ushur platform to provide instant, persistent, two-way text based workflows. Our solutions are customized by the Enterprise; designed for the mobile user, and work seamlessly on top of existing service delivery platforms like ServiceNow.

Deploying Ushur micro-engagements dramatically reduces costs by eliminating incoming calls to the service-desk and replacing them with self-service workflows.
